About Us

OUR INSTITUTE

Government Engineering College Challakere was established in 2021 under the Department of collegiate and Technical Education, Karnataka with the objective of providing quality technical education to rural students. Government engineering college has evolved into an institution of excellence in teaching students to meet industrial needs, consultation services to society and training programs through eminent faculties. At present institute offers 3 courses viz., ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND MACHINE LEARNING, AUTOMOBILE ENGINEERING & COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING. All these three branches approved by AICTE and Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University Belagavi. Ours is the only institute having advanced course like ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND MACHINE LEARNING among Government engineering colleges Karnataka.
